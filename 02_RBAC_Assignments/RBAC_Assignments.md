## Built-in Roles Used

### Reader
- Assigned to HR Team
- Scope: Resource Group
- Access: View-only

### Contributor
- Assigned to Developers
- Scope: Resource Group
- Access: Create and manage resources

### Owner
- Assigned to IT Admins
- Scope: Subscription
- Access: Full control including role assignments

## Why RBAC?
RBAC ensures the principle of least privilege and secure access control.
